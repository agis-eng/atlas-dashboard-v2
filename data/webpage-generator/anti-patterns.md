# Anti-Patterns

Reject or rewrite drafts with these traits:

## Generic structure problems
- hero with only buzzwords and no concrete offer
- three feature cards pretending to be a full page
- CTA appears once and too late
- every section has the same layout and weight
- no proof, credibility, or trust signals

## Generic visual problems
- default purple-blue SaaS gradients with no reason
- untouched shadcn demo styling
- too many identical rounded cards
- weak headline hierarchy
- no signature visual move
- decorative motion that adds noise, not clarity

## Generic copy problems
- words like seamless, innovative, cutting-edge without substance
- generic claims without evidence
- paragraphs that never explain who the offer is for
- CTA text that does not match the service or user intent

## Dashboard-specific failures
- flat gray surfaces with no visual priority
- every metric card looks equally important
- tables dominate without summary context
- action hierarchy is unclear
