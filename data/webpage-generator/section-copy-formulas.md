# Section Copy Formulas

Use these as scaffolding for stronger first drafts.

## Hero
- headline: concrete promise + audience or outcome
- subheadline: what it is, who it is for, why trust it
- CTA: specific next step matching buyer intent
- support line: one proof cue or reassurance close to CTA

## Proof block
- 3-5 credibility items max
- outcomes, clients served, testimonials, certifications, locations, or special expertise
- short labels beat long paragraphs

## Services / capabilities
- name each service clearly
- pair each with a plain-language benefit
- avoid buzzword-only descriptions

## Process / how it works
- 3-5 steps
- each step should reduce uncertainty
- write in buyer language, not internal ops jargon

## FAQ
- answer friction questions about price, timeline, availability, insurance, fit, location, or process
- keep answers direct

## Final CTA
- restate the value
- reduce hesitation
- make the next action obvious
