# Visual Motifs

Use selectively. Pick one or two motifs that reinforce the concept.

## Premium / polished
- soft layered panels
- refined border treatments
- subtle grain or texture
- calm shadow hierarchy
- restrained accent glow

## Modern technical
- grid overlays
- terminal or system framing details
- split panels with contrasting density
- compact proof strips
- code/data-inspired dividers

## Editorial / authority
- oversized headline with narrow body column
- strong crop lines or framed imagery
- serif/sans pairing
- pull-quote or stat callouts
- magazine-like spacing changes between sections

## Local-trust / service
- approachable imagery blocks
- clear phone/contact affordances
- map, location, or service-area framing
- testimonial cards with human identifiers
- trust badges close to CTA

## Motion notes
- use one entrance rhythm for the page
- reserve stronger motion for the hero or CTA moments
- do not animate every element equally
